Some Things About Me

I am a software developer with an love for vegan food. Breaking down different different "comfort food" recipes and recreating them in a vegan way is a passion of mine. I love seeing that form of art come to life. Just like in cooking, in coding I find that I enjoy the art of breaking down a problem and creating a solution. I fell in love with coding about two years ago. I began to teach myself, but quicly learned that I wanted to be in a classroom setting and learn and ask questions. I found Actualize Coding Academy and decided to enroll. While going to Actualize, I learned so much more than I could have ever imagined and created a few projects.
